Services

Comprehensive support from concept to release and beyond.

01

Copyright Services

We assist songwriters with registering their copyrights to protect their intellectual property, ensuring their work is legally safeguarded.

02

ISRC Code Generation

We assign International Standard Recording Codes (ISRC) to tracks for unique identification and accurate tracking across platforms.

03

Music Distribution

We facilitate the digital and physical distribution of music to major streaming platforms and retailers, maximizing exposure for our songwriters.

04

Performance Rights Registration

We register songs with Performance Rights Organizations (PROs) to ensure songwriters receive royalties for public performances.

05

Royalty Collection

We ensure that songwriters receive all forms of royalties, including mechanical, performance, and synchronization royalties.

06

Song Licensing

We negotiate and manage licensing deals for TV shows, films, commercials, and other media, opening doors for new revenue streams.

07

Music Administration

We handle song registrations, catalog management, and global publishing rights to ensure efficient operations and protect songwriters' works.

08

Artist Support & Development

We offer personalized guidance, resources, and mentorship to help songwriters grow their careers and build sustainable futures in music.

09

Collaborative Opportunities

We connect songwriters, producers, and artists for new projects, fostering creativity and expanding musical horizons.

10

Publishing Splits & Agreements

We manage and negotiate fair publishing splits and agreements, ensuring that all parties involved are treated with transparency and respect.
